Teen's Fake Instagram: Koparkhairane Man Arrested

Navi Mumbai police apprehended a 22-year-old Koparkhairane resident for creating a bogus Instagram profile impersonating a local teenager. The accused, identified as [Accused's Name - if available], allegedly posted sexually explicit content and images to harass the victim. The cybercrime unit of the Navi Mumbai police acted on a complaint filed by the teen's parents.

The investigation revealed the accused had been stalking the victim online for several months. He obtained personal details to create the fake profile. The police tracked the IP address to a residence in Sector 12, Koparkhairane.

He now faces charges under the IT Act and relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code. This arrest highlights the growing threat of online harassment targeting young people in the city. The police are urging parents to monitor their children's online activity.
