MI vs DC: DY Patil ready for WPL clash

TLDR: Mumbai Indians Women (MI) face Delhi Capitals Women (DC) at DY Patil Stadium. Expect runs; the pitch is a belter.

Another Women's Premier League (WPL) showdown descends on our very own DY Patil Stadium. Forget your Sunday plans; this is where the action is. MI Women are locking horns with DC Women, and frankly, we're expecting fireworks.

Word on the street (and from Business Standard, apparently) is that the pitch is primed for batting. Translation: sixes galore. DY Patil is known for its pace and bounce. Bowlers, good luck. You'll need it.

Key stats? Who cares! Just kidding (sort of). Expect a high-scoring affair. Maybe someone will finally park one on the Seawoods Grand Central complex. Now that would be a headline.

Tickets are still available, maybe. If you can brave the Seawoods traffic, get down there. Otherwise, find a TV and prepare for some serious hitting. Just don't blame us if your neighbor complains about your cheering.
