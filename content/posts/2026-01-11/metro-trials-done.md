> **TLDR**: Metro Line 1 oscillation trials wrapped up successfully. Is anyone *actually* surprised things went smoothly for once? 

Finally, some good news on the Metro front. Oscillation trials for Line 1 (CBD Belapur to Pendhar) concluded yesterday. That means we're one step closer to *maybe* having a functioning metro system. 

The trials, overseen by the Research Designs and Standards Organisation (RDSO), involved testing the train's performance under various conditions. Think: speed, braking, and the overall smoothness of the ride (or lack thereof).

Meanwhile, residents are cautiously optimistic. The opening of Line 1 promises to ease traffic congestion and provide a much-needed alternative to overcrowded buses and auto-rickshaws. If it ever *actually* opens, that is.
