> **TLDR**: A Navi Mumbai police officer is missing after a letter accusing a senior of corruption surfaced. Someone's career is about to end VERY badly.

The Navi Mumbai police force has a mystery on its hands. Inspector Abhay Kurundkar of the NRI Coastal police station vanished this week, leaving behind a letter alleging widespread corruption within the department.

Kurundkar's wife filed a missing person complaint after he failed to return home. The letter, purportedly written by Kurundkar, names a senior officer, accusing them of demanding kickbacks from local businesses operating near the coast in the Belapur and Vashi areas.

"This isn't just a missing person case anymore," said a source inside the Sector 15 police station. "If that letter checks out, we're looking at a major internal investigation."
