Commuters awaiting buses in Navi Mumbai can now indulge in a spot of reading, courtesy of a novel initiative by the Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC). Select bus shelters across the city have been transformed into miniature libraries, stocked with a diverse collection of books ranging from fiction to non-fiction. 

The initiative, spearheaded by the NMMC's library department, aims to promote reading habits among citizens and make waiting time more productive. The first such 'bus shelter library' was inaugurated near the APMC market in Vashi, Sector 19, with plans to extend the concept to other key locations, including Nerul and CBD Belapur. Initial reports indicate a high rate of engagement, with commuters borrowing books using the provided check-out system.
