Stops include key areas like Mankhurd and the sprawling CIDCO Exhibition Centre, promising to ease travel woes for thousands. Or at least, that's the idea.

The impact? Imagine skipping the Palm Beach Road bottleneck or the Vashi bridge chaos. The new metro aims to be the aorta for airport-bound passengers.
